Process

The Interface design I before I joined is little bit over-fashioned (Screenshoot shown below), like my co-workers said. It was built by Adobe Illustrator for wireframing and IOS UI components as style guide. The user experience process is confusing and the design on screens are not consistent.

The first thing I did as a UI/UX designer is to get a better knowledges on the product and company culture. After that, I had some couple long talks with my co-workers about the researches had been done. Based upon them, I filled up some questionnaires and canvas. I created a site-map of the old app and try to implement my idea.

I used previous user research and design as a reference and start the redesign process. I use Sketch instead of Adobe Illustrator cooperating with Material Design as the style guide with Atomic Design system. The design has being polished all the way from the paper prototype. We kept measuring the product based on the usability testings and developing process, so that we can delivery a most appropriate minimum viable product.
